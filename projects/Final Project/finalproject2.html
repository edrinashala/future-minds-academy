My favorite celebrity





I created this project about my favorite celebrity, Travis Scott, to explore his influence on music and culture.
His unique sound and innovative style inspire me, and I wanted to showcase his journey and achievements.
Through this project, I hope to share my admiration for his artistry and highlight how he has shaped the contemporary music scene.

A local business


I chose to create my project about Albi Mall to highlight its important role in our community.
This shopping destination offers a variety of stores and dining options while serving as a gathering place for families and friends.
By showcasing its unique features, I aim to encourage support for local businesses and demonstrate their impact on our local economy and social life.
